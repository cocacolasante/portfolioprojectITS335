Chicken Francese: The Italian Way

  • Ingredients
  • Instructions
  • Nutritional Facts
  • Best Served With
  • About

Ingredients

  • 1 lb of chicken
  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Paramgiana Cheese
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1 Can of Chicken Broth
  • Quarter cup of White Wine
  • Clove of Garlic
  • 1 Stick of Butter
  • Lemon juice (to taste)
  • Parsley

Instructions

  1. Beat eggs and mix with Parmigana cheese until thick batter
  2. Coat chicken in flour, then dip in batter and then fry until golden brown
  3. Set chicken in oven safe baking tray
  4. Melt butter and mix with white wine and parsley
  5. Bake at 350 degrees for about 30 minutes
  6. Serve with white rice

Nutritional Facts

  • 23 grams of protein
  • 6 grams of fat
  • 8 grams of carbs

Best Served With

While there are many options with this dish, I prefer to serve it with a side of white rice and use the francese butter/wine sauce over the rice and chicken. It can also be served with pasta, commonly Fettuccine, but don't be afraid to mix it up!

About

This dish has a lot of personal meaning. This is a classic dish that my mother made for me growing up and still my favorite dish to this day. This is also a dish I now make with my own son. This dish is best served with rice and broccoli, as that is how my mom made it. I hope all of you enjoy this dish as much as I do!
